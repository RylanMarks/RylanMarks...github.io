The most effective way to censor yourself to it is to simply block, mute, and/or report the player expressing toxic behavior. Toxic behavior could be prevented more effectively in the future if large gaming companies monitored their in-game voice and text chat more and set punishments along with it.

Creative Cocmmons

When someone makes a piece of work, whether it be a video or a song, they have what's called Copyright. Copyright reserves all rights around whatever piece of work to the origanal owner, meaning if you wanted to commercially use a video or song that has copyright, you would need to ask the origanal owner for permission. Luckly there is a website that hosts all pieces of work that the origanal owners put out for free use. Creaative Commons is a global nonprofit organization that has a bunch of songs, images, videos, and more that can be used personally or commercially for free because only some rights are reserved and not all.
